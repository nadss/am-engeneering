Steam Boiler Installations

Industrial Steam Boiler Engineering

AM Engineering designs and fabricates industrial steam boilers to support process heating requirements in distillation plants, food and beverage processing, retort systems, and other thermal applications.

Built on decades of practical engineering experience, our boilers are developed with a focus on operational reliability, safety, and long service life rather than unnecessary complexity.

Boiler Types Manufactured

AM Engineering manufactures both vertical and horizontal fire-tube steam boilers, selected based on client capacity requirements, available space, and operating conditions.

These boilers are suitable for small to medium industrial steam demand and are commonly integrated with distillation columns, retorts, and process vessels supplied by the company.

Construction & Design

Each boiler is fabricated using heavy-gauge carbon steel pressure vessels with reinforced cylindrical shells and full-penetration welded joints. Furnace and fire chambers are designed to accommodate refractory lining where required.

Structural frames are fabricated to provide stable mounting, inspection access, and ease of maintenance in industrial environments.

Safety & Instrumentation

All steam boilers are equipped with essential safety and monitoring components, including pressure gauges, safety valves, steam outlet valves, and water level indicators. These features ensure safe operation and allow operators to monitor boiler conditions during service.

Fuel Flexibility

Boiler furnace designs support a range of firing methods depending on customer requirements and fuel availability. Designs allow for solid fuel, biomass, or oil-fired operation, making the systems adaptable to local operating conditions.
