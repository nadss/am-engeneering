Filling Machine Projects

Industrial Filling Machine Engineering

AM Engineering, formerly known as Auto Motors, designs and manufactures custom filling machines for liquid products used in food, beverage, and process-based industries. Each filling system is engineered with a focus on accuracy, reliability, and ease of operation.

Our filling machines are built to suit specific product characteristics, container sizes, and production requirements, ensuring consistent filling performance and long-term operational stability.

Filling Machine Capabilities

Based on application requirements, AM Engineering manufactures vacuum-based and semi-automatic filling systems suitable for low to medium viscosity liquids. These systems are designed to deliver uniform fill levels while minimizing product wastage.

Multi-head configurations allow simultaneous filling of multiple containers, improving productivity without compromising accuracy.

Key Design Features

  • Multi-Head Filling Nozzles
  • Consistent Fill Level Control
  • Vacuum-Assisted Operation
  • Adjustable Bottle Height Support

Construction & Materials

All product-contact components are fabricated using food-grade stainless steel and hygienic tubing materials. Structural frames are engineered for durability and vibration resistance, ensuring stable performance even under continuous operation.

The machines are designed for easy cleaning, straightforward maintenance, and operator-friendly use, making them suitable for both small-scale and medium-scale production environments.

Why Choose AM Engineering

With more than five decades of hands-on engineering experience, AM Engineering delivers filling machines that prioritize practical performance over complexity. Clients benefit from robust construction, reliable operation, and long-term technical support.
