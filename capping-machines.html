Capping Machine Projects

Capping Machine Engineering

AM Engineering designs and manufactures reliable capping machines for use in bottling and packaging operations. Our capping equipment is developed with a focus on mechanical simplicity, repeatability, and long-term serviceability.

Drawing on decades of hands-on engineering experience, we build capping machines that are suited for real production environments rather than laboratory or display use. Each machine is designed to operate consistently with minimal adjustment and downtime.

Types of Capping Machines

Our capping solutions include semi-automatic and auto-lifting capping machines, capable of handling a variety of bottle sizes and cap types. Machines can be configured as standalone units or integrated into a complete bottling line.

Capping Head Design

AM Engineering manufactures its own capping heads, allowing precise control over torque, alignment, and repeatability. Multiple head configurations can be supplied depending on cap type, including plastic caps and other common closures.

  • Interchangeable Capping Heads
  • Suitable for Local Production Lines
  • Durable Mechanical Construction

Engineering Approach

Our capping machines are engineered with an emphasis on robustness and ease of maintenance. Rather than excessive automation, we focus on practical mechanical solutions that can be serviced locally and operated reliably over long periods of time.

This approach has made AM Engineering a trusted supplier of capping equipment for small to mid-scale manufacturers seeking dependable and cost-effective machinery.
