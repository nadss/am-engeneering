Welcome to

AM Engineering

AM Engineering, formerly known as Auto Motors, is a Sri Lankan industrial engineering company with more than five decades of hands-on experience in mechanical fabrication and machinery manufacturing.

We specialize in the design, fabrication, installation, and servicing of custom-built industrial equipment for food processing, beverage production, and general manufacturing industries. Our products include distillation plants, filling and capping machines, steam boilers, retorts, industrial tanks, and fully customized process equipment.
