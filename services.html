Our Capabilities

Engineering Solutions

Distillation Plants

We design and fabricate custom distillation plants covering system layout, fabrication, installation, and commissioning. Each plant is engineered for process stability, thermal efficiency, and long-term operational reliability.

Bottling Machines

Our bottling machines are designed for consistent filling accuracy, smooth operation, and ease of maintenance. Systems are customized based on bottle type, production volume, and product characteristics.

Capping Machines

We manufacture custom capping machines designed to provide uniform sealing across different bottle sizes and cap types, with a focus on durability, mechanical simplicity, and operator safety.

Pumps

We fabricate and assemble industrial pump systems for fluid transfer applications, including food-grade and process liquids, configured to meet specific flow, pressure, and operating conditions.

Retorts

Our industrial retorts are engineered for controlled pressure and temperature applications, with careful attention to structural integrity, sealing performance, and operational safety.

Steam Boilers

We design and fabricate steam boilers to support industrial heating and process requirements, focusing on safe operation, thermal efficiency, and robust construction for continuous use.
